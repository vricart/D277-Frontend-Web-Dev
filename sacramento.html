Sacramento

City of Trees

Sacramento

capital of the U.S. state of California, lies at the confluence of the Sacramento River and American River. The district of Old Sacramento harkens back to the city's Gold Rush era, with wooden sidewalks and wagon rides. One of several museums in Old Sacramento, the California State Railroad Museum depicts the construction of the Transcontinental Railroad, one of the country's earliest technological feats.

Landmark & Attraction

California State Capitol building, the hub of the state's government and a historic symbol.

History & Heritage

Sacramento played a pivotal role during the California Gold Rush of the mid-1800s, serving as a major distribution point and commercial center.

Neighborhood Highlight

Midtown, a vibrant, artsy area known for its trendy boutiques, bustling nightlife, and a flourishing food scene.

Local Cuisine & Dining

Sacramento is celebrated as the 'Farm-to-Fork Capital', featuring fresh, locally-sourced dishes from its abundant agricultural surroundings.

Sport Team

Sacramento Kings, the city's beloved NBA team that brings thrilling basketball action to the Golden 1 Center.

Famous Person

Joan Didion, an acclaimed author and journalist, known for her sharp insights into California culture and politics.
