San Francisco

officially the City and County of San Francisco, is a commercial, financial, and cultural center within Northern California. With a population of 808,988 residents as of 2023, San Francisco is the fourth-most populous city in the U.S. state of California and the 17th-most populous in the U.S.

Quick Facts

  • Population:

    808,988

  • Incorporated:

    April 15, 1850

  • Region:

    San Francisco Bay Area

  • Classification:

    Urban

  • Median Household Income:

    $78,378 (State Median: $95,521)

Landmark & Attraction

Golden Gate Bridge, an iconic symbol of the city and one of the most photographed bridges in the world.

History & Heritage

San Francisco was a major port and gateway during the California Gold Rush in the mid-1800s.

Neighborhood Highlight

Haight-Ashbury, known for its vibrant history as the epicenter of the 1960s counterculture movement.

Local Cuisine & Dining

San Francisco is famous for its sourdough bread, a culinary tradition dating back to the Gold Rush era.

Sport Team

San Francisco Giants, the city's beloved MLB team that plays at the scenic Oracle Park.

Famous Person

Robin Williams, the legendary comedian and actor who called San Francisco home.
